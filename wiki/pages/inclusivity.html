Inclusivity

In a country as vast and varied as India, no one solution fits all. We quickly realized that to make our E. Solei project truly impactful, we needed to embrace the rich cultural, linguistic, and geographical diversity of the nation. As one team member aptly put it, “Understanding the unique challenges faced by different regions is not just important—it’s essential for designing solutions that work for everyone.” With this belief guiding us, we took deliberate steps to ensure our project was as inclusive and accessible as possible, reaching people across different states, languages, and socio-economic backgrounds.

1. Exploring the Terrain: Understanding Regional Challenges

Understanding that foot health varies across environments, we expanded our interviews to different states in India to capture regional challenges.

Jammu and Kashmir Insights

In Jammu and Kashmir, cold climates necessitate wearing shoes indoors for warmth, causing foot moisture issues. These insights have helped tailor region-specific solutions for footwear needs in colder environments.

West Bengal Insights

In West Bengal, where corporate professionals wear formal shoes as part of their daily routine, the importance of comfort and foot health in office environments was highlighted.

Kerala Insights

Kerala's humid climate contributes to a high incidence of fungal infections. We developed moisture-wicking footwear solutions specific to this region.

Gujarat Insights

In Gujarat's coastal regions, humidity and salt exposure affect foot health. We explored materials that are resistant to these conditions.

2. Understanding Athletes' Footwear Needs: Insights from Coaches and Athletes

We turned our attention to athletes—individuals whose feet bear the brunt of immense strain on a daily basis. Interviews with athletic coaches and athletes revealed the importance of proper footwear for performance and injury prevention.

3. Ek Pehel Outreach: Bringing Science to Underprivileged Children

We partnered with the Ek Pehel initiative at IISER Kolkata to provide education to underprivileged children, ensuring that science education reaches those with limited resources.

4. Embracing Linguistic Diversity: Translating Surveys for Inclusivity

We translated our surveys into Kashmiri, Malayalam, Bengali, Punjabi, and Gujarati to ensure broader accessibility across linguistic barriers.

5. Celebrating LGBTQ+ Contributions to Science

During Pride Month, we celebrated the remarkable contributions of LGBTQ+ individuals in science through our social media platforms.
